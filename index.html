About Me.

I'm Dinesh R, a Game Developer specializing in Unreal Engine 5, with a focus on gameplay programming, level design, AI systems, and cinematic experiences. I enjoy turning ideas into interactive gameplay by building mechanics, designing engaging environments, and creating systems that feel responsive and enjoyable to play.

My experience includes developing multiplayer and single-player games using Blueprints and C++, implementing AI behaviors, UI systems, level progression, and gameplay mechanics. I've also created cinematic sequences using UE5 Sequencer, working with custom environments, lighting, and camera animation to produce high-quality real-time visuals. Alongside Unreal Engine, I have experience developing 2D games in Unity with C#.

I'm continuously expanding my skills by building personal projects, experimenting with new gameplay systems, and exploring modern game development workflows. My goal is to create polished, immersive experiences while collaborating with teams to bring creative ideas to life.
